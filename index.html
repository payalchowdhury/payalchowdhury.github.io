I'm Payal — a product design and strategy lead who believes the best work lives at the intersection of rigorous thinking and genuine creativity. I've spent over a decade leading creative programs and driving the end-to-end delivery of over 100 products across 10+ brands and global teams. I specialise in managing complex creative pipelines that span both physical and digital touch points. What excites me most is helping teams build products that people admire and actually want to use.

I've worked across three continents with startups, scale-ups, and enterprises — always brought clarity where there's ambiguity, and momentum where things have stalled.

I create — frameworks, tools, and resources that distil complex ideas into something clear and actionable.

Currently open to consulting, collaborations, and speaking engagements globally.
