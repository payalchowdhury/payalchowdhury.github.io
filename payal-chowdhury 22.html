(01)
Aura — AI-Powered Appliance UX
Redesigning the intelligence layer of a connected kitchen appliance — making AI recommendations feel intuitive, not intrusive. NPS lifted from 34 to 71.
AI/UXConsumer Electronics2025
2025
→
Problem

Smart appliances were getting smarter — but users were ignoring AI suggestions entirely. A connected oven with 40+ automated features had a feature-adoption rate below 12%. The AI felt alien, not helpful.

My Role

End-to-end UX lead — user research, interaction design, prototyping, testing, and delivery handoff across a 6-month engagement.

Process

Ran 24 contextual interviews in home kitchens across three cities. Identified a core insight: users didn't distrust the AI — they distrusted the timing of its suggestions. Built a new interaction model where AI nudges appear at natural pause points, not mid-task. Introduced a confidence-transparency layer — the appliance shows why it's making a suggestion.

Outcome
+108%Feature adoption
71NPS (from 34)
-40%Support tickets
(02)
Halo — Smart Home Control System
Unified IoT platform for a 12-device smart home ecosystem — replacing 6 separate apps with one adaptive interface. Retention up 63% in 90 days.
IoTSmart Home2025
2025
→
Problem

A hardware manufacturer had built 12 smart home products across 3 years — each with its own app. Users managing multiple devices experienced severe app fatigue. The ecosystem had no coherent identity, and cross-device automation was essentially unusable.

My Role

Strategy and design lead — defined the unified platform vision, led a team of 4 designers, and drove alignment across hardware, software, and marketing stakeholders.

Process

Started with a 3-week discovery sprint mapping all device interactions and user mental models across existing apps. Built a unified design language with adaptive layouts — the interface reorganises itself based on time-of-day and usage patterns. Introduced a "Scene" model allowing users to control 8 devices with a single tap. Ran 6 rounds of usability testing before launch.

Outcome
+63%90-day retention
6→1Apps consolidated
4.8★App store rating
(03)
Pulse — AI Health & Wellness App
Designing trust into an AI health companion — turning passive tracking into meaningful behavioural change. 41% increase in daily active use within 60 days of launch.
AI/HealthMobile2024
2024
→
Problem

Health apps were excellent at collecting data and terrible at changing behaviour. Users would open the app for a week then abandon it entirely. The AI surfaced insights users couldn't act on — technically accurate, humanly useless.

My Role

Product design and strategy lead — 0 to 1 build including research, UX architecture, AI recommendation logic (with ML engineers), visual design, and accessibility audit.

Process

Used a Jobs-to-be-Done framework across 30 interviews to distinguish between what users tracked vs what they cared about. Redesigned the AI layer to surface one meaningful nudge per day, timed to the user's natural routine. Built a progressive disclosure model — depth available to those who want it, invisible to those who don't. All AI language was co-developed with a behavioural psychologist to pass WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility standards.

Outcome
+41%Daily active use
WCAG AAAccessibility
+28%30-day retention
(04)
Forma — AI Design System
A token-based, AI-assisted design system for connected product teams — reducing time-to-first-prototype from 3 weeks to 4 days across a 60-person org.
Design SystemsAI Tooling2024
2024
→
Problem

A 60-person product org was shipping inconsistent UX across hardware companion apps, web dashboards, and embedded touchscreen interfaces. Design debt had accumulated over 4 years, slowing every sprint and frustrating engineers who were rebuilding the same components from scratch for each platform.

My Role

Design system strategy lead and founding designer — audited the existing UI landscape, defined the token architecture, and built the governance model for the system.

Process

Audited 1,400 UI components across 8 products — identified 62% duplication. Built Forma on a 4-tier token system (primitive → semantic → component → platform) with AI-assisted variant generation to accelerate component creation. Embedded the system into the CI/CD pipeline so misuse triggers automated Figma annotations. Trained 4 design teams across 3 time zones on adoption.

Outcome
-85%Time to prototype
62%Component duplication eliminated
3×Faster design QA
(05)
Setu — Hardware Onboarding System
Reimagining the out-of-box experience for a complex IoT device — reducing setup abandonment from 47% to 9% through contextual guidance and adaptive flows.
OnboardingHardware UX2023
2023
→
Problem

Nearly half of all purchased IoT devices were being returned or abandoned before completing setup. The onboarding flow assumed technical literacy that most users didn't have — 14 steps, no progress indication, zero error recovery. The product was good. The experience of getting to it was broken.

My Role

UX strategy and design lead — owned the full onboarding experience from unboxing to first meaningful use, working alongside firmware engineers and industrial designers.

Process

Conducted 18 unboxing studies — observational sessions in users' homes, recording where confusion and abandonment occurred. Found that 71% of drop-offs happened at a single WiFi pairing screen. Rebuilt the entire flow around a 3-step model: Plug → Pair → Personalise. Used adaptive guidance that detects failure states and offers contextual help before users know they need it. Designed for one-thumb use and low-literacy contexts for accessibility.

Outcome
47%→9%Setup abandonment
-34%Returns & refunds
+22ptsNPS at setup
